Life Story

Albert Kenrick Fisher was born in Sing Sing, New York and grew up on a hill near the Hudson River.

A founding member of the American Ornithologists' Union in 1883, he was president of the group from 1914-1917. He was a member of Bird-Lore's Advisory Council
